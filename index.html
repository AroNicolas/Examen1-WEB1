ABOUT US

Established in the heart of the city, our club embodies the spirit of
competition, camaraderie, and passion for the game. Since our
inception in 2005, we have been dedicated to nurturing talent and
fostering a love for football. Our experienced coaches are commited
to not only developing technical skills but also building character
and resilience in every member.
